3 days 2 nights Hanoi – Ha Long Excursion

From 182$

Highlight

In this tour, we will have 3 days to explore landmarks in Hanoi and Ha Long such as Hoan Kiem Lake, The Huc Bridge, Temple of Literature, visit caves and tourist attractions in the bay, and enjoy regional specialties, and street food from both cities.

Itinerary

Day 1

Pick up guests at the airport, then go sightseeing and explore landmarks in Hanoi such as Hoan Kiem Lake, Temple of Literature, Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum, Tran Quoc Pagoda, guests will have the opportunity to enjoy the specialties of Hanoi as well as the North at local restaurants.

Day 2

Depart for Ha Long Bay, take a boat to visit the bay and caves such as Sung Sot Cave, Luon Cave, visit Titov Island, visit Cua Van fishing village. Walk and shop at Ha Long night market.

Day 3

Continue the tour in Ha Long, go swimming and visit Quang Ninh Museum before returning to Hanoi.

Meals

Day 1: (L,D) at local restaurant

Day 2: (B,L,D) have lunch at seaside restaurant and have dinner on the yacht with art performances

Day 3: (B,L) buffet at hotel’s restaurant and have lunch at local restaurant

Accommodation

Ha Noi: Mayflower Hotel - a hotel in the center of Hanoi

Ha Long: Muong Thanh Luxury Hotel

Services

Ha Long: kayaking, participating in fun activities at Dragon Park, Ha Long Park, riding the Queen's cable car, giant Sunwheel

In addition, AST also provides guests with helicopter service to visit Tuan Chau Island, beaches, diving or canoeing. Guests can also sit on a 5-star cruise to sightsee Ha Long Bay at night, have dinner and enjoy art performances and parties on the cruise.
